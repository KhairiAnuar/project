Dog



Dogs often have jobs, including as police dogs, army dogs, assistance dogs, fire dogs, messenger dogs, hunting dogs, herding dogs, or rescue dogs

Dogs like to be petted, but only when they can first see the petter's hand before petting; one should never pet a dog from behind

Dogs often chase cats, and most dogs will fetch a ball or stick

There are guard dogs, hunting dogs, herding dogs, guide dogs for blind people, and police dogs

Dogs whose parents were the same breed will also be that breed: these dogs are called purebred or pure pedigree dogs

Dogs with parents from different breeds no longer belong to one breed: they are called mutts, mixed-breed dogs, hybrids, or mongrels

These kinds of dogs, instead of being called mutts, are known as designer dog breeds
